» A summary of what happened.

Oligotyping analysis was performed on 98 reads from 10 samples for "(Geodermatophilaceae)-oligotyping-1474656828888" with oligotyping pipeline version 2.1 (available from https://meren.github.io/projects/oligotyping/) using 2 components automatically selected form the highest entropy values following the initial entropy analysis. To reduce the noise, each oligotype required to (1) appear in at least 2 samples, (2) occur in more than 0.0% of the reads for at least one sample, (3) represent a minimum of 0 reads in all samples combined, and (4) have a most abundant unique sequence with a minimum abundance of 0. Oligotypes that did not meet these criteria were removed from the analysis. The final number of quality controlled oligotypes revealed by the analysis was 4, and they represented 98 reads, which was equivalent to 100.00% of all reads analyzed.
