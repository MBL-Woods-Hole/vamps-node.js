Overview

» A summary of what happened.

Oligotyping analysis was performed on 60 reads from 4 samples for "(Comamonadaceae)-oligotyping-1475002307139" with oligotyping pipeline version 2.1 (available from https://meren.github.io/projects/oligotyping/) using 2 components following the initial entropy analysis. To reduce the noise, each oligotype required to (1) appear in at least 2 samples, (2) occur in more than 0.0% of the reads for at least one sample, (3) represent a minimum of 0 reads in all samples combined, and (4) have a most abundant unique sequence with a minimum abundance of 0. Oligotypes that did not meet these criteria were removed from the analysis. The final number of quality controlled oligotypes revealed by the analysis was 1, and they represented 58 reads, which was equivalent to 96.67% of all reads analyzed.
