Portals

Portals are areas of the site dedicated to specific umbrella projects or initiatives that contain many VAMPS projects.
They typically involve more than one primary investigator and strive to make the data publicly available although some of the data may still be private.



  • MoBE (Microbiology of the Built Environment)
  • ICoMM (International Census of Marine Mirobes)
  • CoDL (Census of Deep Life)
  • HMP (Human Microbiome Project)
  • UC (Ulcerative Colitis)
  • The Rare Biosphere
  • CMP (Coral Microbe Project)
  • MIRADA (Microbial Inventory Research Across Diverse Aquatic Long Term Ecological Research Sites)
